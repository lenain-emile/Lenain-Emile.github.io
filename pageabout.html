About me

I was born in Marseille (France) on March 8, 2005. My educational background is rather classical, I obtained my baccalaureate in math and economic and social science. During my last year of elementary school, I became interested in the booming IT sector, where I found some pleasure in coding. What I like most is the creativity that web development requires. My temperament is rather objective, as I prioritize the ideas that will really advance my project, rather than my tastes and preferences. I'm also very open-minded, which means I can work with all kinds of profiles. Whoever you work with will be a pleasure.
